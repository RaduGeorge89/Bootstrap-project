Did you know?

In 2010, a team of archaeologists discovered what is believed to be the world's oldest known brewery in a cave in Israel. The site dates back to around 13,000 years ago and contained evidence of a beer production process involving the fermentation of grains, possibly for religious or communal purposes. This finding provides fascinating insights into the ancient origins of beer and its role in human culture.
